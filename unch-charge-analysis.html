Wrangle

Lets organize Payor columns into a more manageable format.

charges_cln <- charges %>%
  # Wrangle payor columns to key:value pair
  pivot_longer(
    cols = aetna:united_healthcare_medicare_advantage,
    names_to = "payor",
    values_to = "charge"
    )

Looks like most our charge data is not numeric.
